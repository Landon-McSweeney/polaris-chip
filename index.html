Volkswagen is a German automobile manufacturer known for making fun small drivers cars like the Golf GTI and the Jetta GLI.

Audi is a premium German car brand known for their Quattro all wheel drive systems and RS models.

Lamborghini is an Italian sports car brand known for high-performance supercars.

Land Rover specializes in luxury SUVs with off-road capabilities they are also well known for the classic Defender 90 and 110.

Bugatti is a high-performance car brand famous for speed and engineering excellence.
